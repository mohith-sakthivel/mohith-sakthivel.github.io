I am a post baccalaureate fellow at Robert Borch Centre of Data Science and AI (RBCDSAI) located within IIT-Madras. I am currently working on Deep Reinforcement Learning under the guidance of Prof. Nandan Sudarsanam and Prof. Balaraman Ravindran.

My research revolves around areas such exploration through intrinsic motivation, abstraction, and meta-reinforcement learning. Lately, I've also been thinking a lot about causal and bayesian reasoning and their role in building intelligent systems.

Previously, I worked at ABB as a Robotics Engineer. I did my undergrad in mechanical engineering at PSG College of Technology. I also spent a semester on exchange at RMIT University.

Feel free to write to me if you wish to know more about my research or to chat about other exciting ideas.
