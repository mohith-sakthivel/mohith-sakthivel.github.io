Resources

There are a lot of wonderful resources on the internet to learn about AI - ranging from lecture recordings to twitter threads. Here, I have complied some of the stuff that I have thoroughly enjoyed and wish to recommend to others.

Feel free to drop me an e-mail if you have any recommendations that could potentially make into this list.

Deep Learning

  • Information Theory of Deep Learning - Prof. Naftali Tishby (Lecture)
  • Understanding LSTM Networks - Christopher Olah (Blog)
  • Deep Learning Lecture Series 2020 - DeepMind x UCL (Lectures)

Deep Reinforcement Learning

  • Spinning Up in Deep RL - Josh Achiam (GitHub Repo/Blog)
  • Pong from Pixels (in 130 lines of code) - Andrej Karpathy (GitHub Repo/Blog)
  • Understanding RL Vision - Chris Olah (Blog)
  • What Matters for On-Policy Deep Actor-Critic Methods? A Large-Scale Study (Paper)

Research/Academia

  • An Opinionated Guide to ML Research - John Schulman (Blog)
  • Lessons from My First Two Years of AI Research - Tom Silver (Blog)

Others
